A hands on experience for students!

West-Mec (Western Maricopa Education Center), is a public school district that provides innovative career and technical education (CTE) programs that prepare students to enter the workforce and pursue continuing education. Students will learn from experienced teachers who have worked in the field.

What is Dental Assisting?

The Dental Assisting program provides a in-depth training for a future career in the dental industry. Students will learn how to assist medical proffesionals with:

  • Dental examinations
  • sterilize and prepare instruments
  • Polish teeth
  • Develope X-rays
  • Take impressions of teeth
